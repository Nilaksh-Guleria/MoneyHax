About Cryptocurrency and Blockchain

Cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual currency that uses cryptography for security. It operates on a decentralized network based on blockchain technology, a distributed ledger that records all transactions across a network of computers.

Why Hold Cryptocurrency?

Holding cryptocurrency offers potential investment opportunities, diversification in portfolios, and participation in innovative technologies shaping the future.

It provides a hedge against inflation, access to decentralized finance (DeFi), and an opportunity to be part of a global financial revolution.
